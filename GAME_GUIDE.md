# 🎮 StickRealm Game Guide

Welcome to StickRealm! This guide will help you master the arena.

## 🎯 Getting Started

### Character Creation

1. **Choose Your Callsign** - Your warrior name (max 20 characters)
2. **Select Your Skin** - Pick from 6 vibrant colors
3. **Choose Gear** - Customize with accessories:
   - None - Classic look
   - Top Hat - Sophisticated warrior
   - Glasses - Tactical vision
4. **Select Game Mode** - Choose your battle type
5. **Bot Settings** - Toggle AI opponents

### First Match Tips

- **Start with Practice Mode** to learn mechanics
- **Stay near power-ups** for advantages
- **Watch the kill feed** to track threats
- **Use the minimap** to avoid ambushes

## ⚔️ Combat System

### Basic Attack (SPACE)
- **Cooldown:** 1.2 seconds
- **Range:** 80 units
- **Cone:** 60 degrees in front
- **Damage:** 12 + (Level × 2)
- **Tip:** Time your attacks! Spamming won't work

### Attack Mechanics

**Lunge System**
- Each attack lunges you forward 20 units
- Use this for gap closing
- Can be used to dodge backwards attacks

**Hit Detection**
- Targets must be in front of you (60° cone)
- Distance matters - get close!
- Angle your character toward enemies

**Damage Calculation**
```
Base Damage = 12 + (Your Level × 2)
+ Damage Buff: ×1.5
+ Rage: ×1.3
- Enemy Shield: ×0.5
- Enemy Guard: ×0.2
```

## 🌟 Skills

### 1. Dash (SHIFT)
- **Cooldown:** 3 seconds
- **Distance:** 150 units
- **Use Cases:**
  - Close distance to enemies
  - Escape dangerous situations
  - Dodge heavy attacks
  - Chase fleeing opponents
- **Pro Tip:** Dash through enemies to confuse them

### 2. Heavy Slash (Q)
- **Cooldown:** 8 seconds
- **Range:** 100 units (larger than basic attack)
- **Damage:** 40 + (Level × 5)
- **Use Cases:**
  - Finish low-HP enemies
  - Hit multiple targets
  - Break through defenses
- **Pro Tip:** Combo with Rage for massive damage

### 3. Guard (E)
- **Cooldown:** 12 seconds
- **Duration:** 3 seconds
- **Effect:** Reduce incoming damage by 80%
- **Use Cases:**
  - Survive when low HP
  - Tank damage for your team
  - Bait enemy cooldowns
  - Escape from multiple attackers
- **Pro Tip:** Use when surrounded, then Dash away

### 4. Rage (R)
- **Cooldown:** 25 seconds
- **Duration:** 6 seconds
- **Effects:**
  - +30% damage
  - +30% movement speed
  - Red aura visual
- **Use Cases:**
  - Secure kills
  - Turn the tide of battle
  - Chase down enemies
  - 1v1 duels
- **Pro Tip:** Combine with Heavy Slash for devastating combos

## 💎 Power-ups

### Health Pack (Red Cube)
- **Effect:** Restore 40 HP
- **Respawn:** 15 seconds
- **Strategy:** Control health pack locations

### Speed Boost (Cyan Octahedron)
- **Effect:** +70% movement speed
- **Duration:** 6 seconds
- **Strategy:** Use for repositioning or chasing

### Damage Buff (Orange Torus)
- **Effect:** +50% damage
- **Duration:** 8 seconds
- **Strategy:** Combo with skills for maximum damage

### Shield (Blue Sphere)
- **Effect:** -50% incoming damage
- **Duration:** 8 seconds
- **Strategy:** Push aggressively while shielded

## 🎮 Game Modes

### ⚔️ Deathmatch
**Objective:** First to 20 kills wins

**Strategy:**
- Control power-up spawns
- Target weak enemies
- Avoid 1v2+ situations
- Use the environment for cover
- Track the leaderboard

**Tips:**
- Steal kills when enemies are low
- Don't chase too far
- Respawn quickly and re-engage

### 🛡️ Team Battle
**Objective:** Team with most kills wins

**Strategy:**
- Stick with teammates
- Focus fire on single targets
- Protect low-HP allies
- Control map sections
- Communicate with positioning

**Tips:**
- Red team spawns on one side, Blue on the other
- Team colors override character colors
- Coordinate skill usage
- Don't leave teammates behind

### 💀 Survival
**Objective:** Be the last one standing

**Strategy:**
- Play defensively early
- Let others fight first
- Conserve skills for final fights
- Position near power-ups
- Avoid the center initially

**Tips:**
- No respawns - every life counts!
- Third-party weakened enemies
- Use Guard to survive longer
- Map awareness is crucial

### 🎯 Practice
**Objective:** Train and improve

**Strategy:**
- Always includes bots
- Test skill combos
- Learn attack timing
- Practice movement
- Experiment with builds

**Tips:**
- No pressure environment
- Perfect for warming up
- Try different playstyles
- Learn bot patterns

## 📊 Leveling System

### XP Gain
- **Kill:** 50 XP
- **Level Up:** Every 100 XP × Current Level

### Level Benefits
- **+20 Max HP** per level
- **+2 Base Damage** per level
- **Full HP Restore** on level up

### Strategy
- Early kills snowball your power
- Higher level = tankier and stronger
- Level advantage is significant
- Don't die and lose momentum

## 🎯 Advanced Tactics

### Movement Techniques

**Strafing**
- Move perpendicular to enemy attacks
- Makes you harder to hit
- Maintain attack angle

**Kiting**
- Attack while moving away
- Effective against melee enemies
- Use with Speed Boost

**Juking**
- Sudden direction changes
- Dodge predictable attacks
- Confuse enemy aim

### Combat Combos

**Aggressive Combo**
```
Rage → Dash → Heavy Slash → Basic Attacks
```
Massive burst damage for securing kills

**Defensive Combo**
```
Guard → Collect Power-ups → Dash Away
```
Survive and reset the fight

**Chase Combo**
```
Speed Boost → Dash → Basic Attack
```
Catch fleeing enemies

**Team Fight Combo**
```
Damage Buff → Heavy Slash → Rage → Basic Attacks
```
Maximum area damage

### Positioning

**High Ground**
- Psychological advantage
- Better visibility
- Easier to escape

**Power-up Control**
- Camp near health packs when low
- Deny enemy power-ups
- Time your collection

**Map Awareness**
- Know where enemies are
- Predict movement patterns
- Use obstacles for cover

## 🤖 Fighting Bots

### Bot Behavior

**Easy Bots**
- Slow reaction time (800ms)
- Simple movement
- Rarely use skills

**Medium Bots**
- Moderate reaction (400ms)
- Better positioning
- Occasional skill usage

**Hard Bots**
- Fast reaction (150ms)
- Smart movement
- Frequent skill usage
- Strategic retreats

### Counter Strategies

**Against Easy Bots**
- Direct confrontation works
- Basic attacks are enough
- Save skills for players

**Against Medium Bots**
- Use movement to confuse
- Bait their attacks
- Punish cooldowns

**Against Hard Bots**
- Treat like skilled players
- Use full combo potential
- Team up if possible
- Respect their damage
